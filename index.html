Exploring the Design of my Favorite Games

In my many years of playing and developing games, I've always taken note of how games I enjoy are designed. Some of these games make some very smart design decisions that I believe other games, from myself and others, can learn from. On this website, I'd like to discuss some of the design takeaways from some of my favorite games.

Short Descriptions

Oneshot

A top-down rpg where you only have one shot.

Phasmophobia

A procedural game about investigating ghosts in haunted areas.

Subnautica

A survival game where you are the sole survivor of a human ship that crashed on an alien ocean planet.
