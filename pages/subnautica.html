Subnautica

Image credit: WallpaperAccess

When the developers were designing Subnautica, they wanted to create a game without violence and weapons, to resist what they saw as a trend toward violence in video games. However, this decision would actually lead some people to classify this game as partly a horror game. Players best hope of surviving dangerous fauna is not to fight back, but to run away. This design, in combination with vast, dark areas in the game, create a strong sense of dread when entering unknown areas not often present in survival games.
